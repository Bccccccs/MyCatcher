Problem Description

You are given three integers.

Determine whether exactly two of the integers are equal and the remaining one is different.

If exactly two numbers are the same, output “Yes”. Otherwise, output “No”.

⸻

Input Format

The input consists of three integers:

A B C

⸻

Constraints

1 ≤ A, B, C ≤ 9

⸻

Output Format

Output “Yes” if exactly two of the integers are equal; otherwise output “No”.
