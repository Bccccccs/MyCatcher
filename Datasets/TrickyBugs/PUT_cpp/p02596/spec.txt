
You are given a positive integer N.

Starting from the number 7, repeatedly append the digit 7 to the right to form numbers like:

7, 77, 777, 7777, …

Determine the smallest positive integer K such that the number consisting of K digits ‘7’ is divisible by N.

If no such K exists, output -1.

⸻

Input Format

The input contains a single integer:

N

⸻

Constraints

1 <= N <= 10^18

⸻

Output Format

Output a single integer:
	•	the smallest K such that the number made of K consecutive ‘7’ digits is divisible by N, or
	•	-1 if such a K does not exist.
