Problem Description

Two players play a turn-based game.

Player Takahashi starts with A health points, and player Aoki starts with C health points.
On Takahashi’s turn, he deals B damage to Aoki.
On Aoki’s turn, he deals D damage to Takahashi.

Takahashi attacks first.
The players alternate turns until one player’s health becomes zero or less.

Determine whether Takahashi wins the game.

Input Format

The input contains four integers:

A B C D

Constraints

1 ≤ A, B, C, D ≤ 10^9

Output Format

Output “Yes” if Takahashi wins, otherwise output “No”.
